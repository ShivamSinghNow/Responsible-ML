Our Mission

Responsible ML engages students of every skill level in real-life machine learning applications. We are a subsidiary of IHS Computer Science Club. Beginners can attend workshops and learn from other members of the club. More experienced members are invited to participate in community-oriented projects, working with local businesses to implement AI software free of charge. Members of all skill levels are invited to join our frequent hackathons and take advantage of our affiliation with Apollo Hacks. Check out some of our work here!

Projects

> Safe Driving AI: A project designed to ensure drivers pay attention to (eg look at) the road. Avoid distracted driving! > Hair cut predictor: Using an iPhone camera to scan someone's head, the app determines the best haircut for them. See our social media for pictures and live updates!
